# ContentForge — API Cost & Budget Reference

## Overview

This document breaks down the operational costs for running ContentForge's
automated content pipeline. All services use pay-as-you-go pricing, keeping
costs predictable and scalable.

---

## Component 1: Text Generation

Service: Google AI
Model: Gemini 1.5 Flash

Pricing:
  Input  (per 1,000 chars): $0.00001875
  Output (per 1,000 chars): $0.000075

Used for: Blog posts, LinkedIn copy, video scripts, agent prompts

---

## Component 2: Image Creation

Service: OpenAI
Model: DALL-E 3 — Standard Quality, 1024x1024

Pricing:
  Per image: ~$0.04

Used for: Blog header images, LinkedIn visuals, video stills

---

## Component 3: Video Generation

Service: Runway Gen-2

Pricing:
  Per 5-second clip: ~$0.25
  Avg per video (4 clips): ~$1.00

Used for: Faceless social media videos, promo clips

---

## Component 4: Voice & Audio

Service: ElevenLabs — Starter Plan

Pricing:
  Subscription: $5.00 / month (covers typical usage volume)

Used for: Video narration, voiceovers

---

## Estimated Cost Per Full Campaign Run

  Text generation:   ~$0.01
  Header image:      ~$0.04
  Video (4 clips):   ~$1.00
  Audio (amortized): ~$0.20
  ─────────────────────────
  Total per run:     ~$1.25 – $2.00
Estimated cost per campaign run: $0.50 - $2.00 depending on API usage
---

## Notes

- Gemini 1.5 Flash has a generous free tier for experimentation
- DALL-E 3 costs scale linearly — budget accordingly for bulk runs
- ElevenLabs Starter gives ~30 min of audio/month, sufficient for most use cases
- Runway credits can be pre-purchased in bundles for better rates
